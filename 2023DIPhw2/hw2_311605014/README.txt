1. Flip
    After compile 
    ./luminosity YOUR_INPUT.bmp YOUR_OUTPUT.bmp gamma
    For example : ./luminosity input1.bmp output1_flip.bmp 0.5
		./luminosity input1.bmp output1_flip.bmp 0.8



2. Resolution
    After compile
    ./sharpness YOUR_INPUT.bmp YOUR_OUTPUT.bmp filter_no
    For example : ./sharpness input1.bmp output1_1.bmp 1
		./sharpness input1.bmp output1_1.bmp 2



3. Scaling
    After compile
    ./denoise YOUR_INPUT.bmp YOUR_OUTPUT.bmp filter_size
    For example : ./denoise input1.bmp output1.bmp 1
		./denoise input1.bmp output1.bmp 2

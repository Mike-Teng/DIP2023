1. Flip
    After compile 
    ./flip YOUR_INPUT.bmp YOUR_OUTPUT.bmp
    For example : ./flip input1.bmp output1_flip.bmp
	./flip test1.bmp test1_flip.bmp
	./flip test2.bmp test2_flip.bmp



2. Resolution
    After compile
    ./resolution YOUR_INPUT.bmp YOUR_OUTPUT_1.bmp YOUR_OUTPUT_2.bmp YOUR_OUTPUT_3.bmp
    For example : ./resolution input1.bmp output1_1.bmp output1_2.bmp output1_3.bmp
	./resolution test1.bmp test1_1.bmp test1_2.bmp test1_3.bmp
	./resolution test2.bmp test2_1.bmp test2_2.bmp test2_3.bmp


3. Scaling
    After compile
    ./scaling YOUR_INPUT.bmp YOUR_OUTPUT_up.bmp YOUR_OUTPUT_down.bmp
    For example : ./scaling input1.bmp output1_up.bmp output1_down.bmp
	./scaling test1.bmp test1_up.bmp test1_down.bmp
	./scaling test2.bmp test2_up.bmp test2_down.bmp
